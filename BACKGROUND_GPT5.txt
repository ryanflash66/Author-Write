BACKGROUND — GPT‑5 Micro‑Tasks

**Role:** Handle tiny, low‑risk cleanups in isolation. Never change public APIs, security‑relevant code, or secrets.

### Examples
- Typo fixes, docstrings, inline comments.
- Minor CSS spacing/typo (no layout overhauls).
- Rename overly generic variables in private scope only.

### Rules
- One tiny change per PR.  
- Label PR `agent:background` and `size:S`.  
- Update `CHANGELOG.md` with a **Changed** or **Fixed** note.
